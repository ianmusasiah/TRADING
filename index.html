📌 Important Notes

  • A candle’s color does NOT always determine its direction alone.
  • Red can be bullish if there’s a long wick below and price reverses up.
  • Green can be bearish if it rejects the top and price falls afterward.
  • Always wait for confirmation after patterns (especially Doji).

📌 Entry Rules:

  •     📌 How to Read Candles (EASY)
    Green Candle: Price went UP (buyers are strong)
    Red Candle: Price went DOWN (sellers are strong)
    Long wick: Rejection or reversal at that price
    Small body, long wick: Sign of reversal (like Pin Bar)
    Doji: Market is confused — don’t rush

  • Enter on the next candle after pattern confirmation.
    Trade only when the candle fully forms (avoid early entries).
    Use 1-minute or 2-minute expiry time for fast results.
    Combine with support/resistance or trend line for better confirmation.

  • 🎯 Entry & Exit Rules
    Entry: Enter after the next candle breaks the high/low of the setup candle.
    Stop Loss: Just outside the wick (safe area).
    Take Profit: Aim for 1:2 Reward or next key level.

  • 📈 When to Enter a Trade:
    UP Trade: Bullish Pin Bar or Bullish Engulfing appears at support.
    DOWN Trade: Bearish Pin Bar or Bearish Engulfing appears at resistance.
    Wait: If Doji or price is unclear.
